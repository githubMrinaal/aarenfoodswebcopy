Introducing our mouth-watering Jhaal Muri Bhel, a beloved Indian street food classic that has a burst of flavours in every morsel. Crafted with care, this delightful snack embodies the essence of Indian street cuisine, combining the zest of tangy spices, the crunch of puffed rice, and the freshness of wholesome ingredients.


This puffed rice variant is infused with the 'jhaal muri' masala that rekindles fond memories of yore. Just add chopped onion, green chillies, roasted peanuts, crunchy sev and a dash of mustard oil, and polish it all off with elan. Optionally, you can even add chopped tomatoes and cucumber if you don't mind some sogginess.


Jhaal Muri is a favourite for those who crave an explosion of taste with a hint of heat. Experience the joy of authentic Indian snacking with our Jhaal Muri, and let its lively flavours transport you to the heart of India's bustling streets. Spice up your life with this irresistible treat!

Indulge in the sweet magic of our Gulab Jamun, a cherished dessert that captures the essence of Indian culinary delight. These soft, golden-brown spheres are a symphony of flavours and aromas, making every bite a savoury experience.


Our Gulab Jamun is lovingly crafted from the finest khoya (milk solids) and gently fried to perfection, ensuring a delectable, melt-in-the-mouth texture. Each dumpling is then soaked in a fragrant rose-scented sugar syrup, infusing it with sweetness and a touch of floral elegance.


Perfect for celebrations, festivals, or simply as a sweet treat to brighten your day, Gulab Jamun embodies the warmth of traditional Indian hospitality. Its irresistible sweetness and rich, milky flavour will induce feelings of ecstasy within.
